- Heroic starts with 3 substats, gains 4th at +12

## 8. Enhancement Roll Mechanics

- Every 3 enhancement levels (+3, +6, +9, +12, +15), a substat receives a roll
- For **Epic** gear: rolls are distributed randomly across all 4 substats
- For **Heroic** gear: the 4th substat (unlocked at +12) gets forced rolls at +12 and +15
- For **Rare** gear: the 3rd substat (unlocked at +9) gets forced rolls at +9, +12, and +15; the 4th substat gets forced rolls at +12 and +15

### Roll Distribution by Rarity

| Rarity | Starting Substats | Rolls at +15 | Forced Roll Points |
|--------|-------------------|---------------|-------------------|
| Epic | 4 | 9 | None (all random) |
| Heroic | 3 | 8 | 4th sub gets rolls at +12, +15 |
| Rare | 2 | 7 | 3rd sub at +9,+12,+15; 4th sub at +12,+15 |
| Good | 1 | 6 | 2nd sub at +6,+9,+12,+15; 3rd sub at +9,+12,+15; 4th sub at +12,+15 |
| Normal | 0 | 5 | 1st sub at +3,+6,+9,+12,+15; etc. |
